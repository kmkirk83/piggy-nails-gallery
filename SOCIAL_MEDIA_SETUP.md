# Nail'd Social Media Setup Guide

## Overview

Social media is your primary customer acquisition channel. This guide covers setup, content strategy, and posting schedules for Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, and Facebook.

---

## Platform Strategy

### Instagram (Primary Platform) 📸
**Why:** Nail art is highly visual, Instagram is where nail enthusiasts hang out
**Goals:** 
- Build community around nail designs
- Drive traffic to website
- Showcase trending designs
- Encourage user-generated content

**Target audience:** Women 18-45, interested in beauty/fashion/DIY

### TikTok (Growth Platform) 🎬
**Why:** Viral potential, younger demographic, algorithm favors new creators
**Goals:**
- Go viral with nail art videos
- Build brand awareness
- Drive traffic from trending sounds
- Reach Gen Z customers

**Target audience:** Women 16-35, interested in trends/DIY

### Pinterest (Traffic Driver) 📌
**Why:** High intent audience, drives website traffic, long content lifespan
**Goals:**
- Drive traffic to website
- Showcase design inspiration
- Build backlinks to product pages
- Long-term organic reach

**Target audience:** Women 25-55, interested in DIY/inspiration

### Facebook (Community) 👥
**Why:** Older demographic, community building, group engagement
**Goals:**
- Build community
- Share behind-the-scenes content
- Customer testimonials
- Longer-form content

**Target audience:** Women 35+, interested in community

**Content Mix (Weekly):**
- 40% Product showcases (nail designs, subscription boxes)
- 30% Educational (nail care tips, design tutorials)

**Week 2:**

### Engagement Strategy

**Daily Activities (15-30 min):**
- Like 20-30 posts from nail art hashtags
- Comment meaningfully on 10-15 posts
- Respond to all comments on your posts
- Follow 5-10 relevant accounts

**Weekly:**
- Host engagement pod (tag 5-10 accounts to engage)
- Run poll/quiz in Stories
- Repost customer content (tag them)

3. **Use trending sounds** (Reels)
   - Check TikTok/Instagram for trending audio

   - Post as Reels

4. **Go live weekly**
   - Live Q&A about nail care
   - Live product launch

## TikTok Setup

### Account Creation

1. **Go to** https://tiktok.com
2. **Create account** with:
   - Username: @nailed or @nailedart
   - Email: xxearthx@gmail.com
3. **Switch to Creator Account**:
   - Settings → Account → Switch to Creator Account
4. **Complete Profile**:
   - Profile photo: Nail'd logo
   - Bio: "Premium nail art 💅 Trending designs • Shop link in bio"
   - Link: https://nailed.app

### Content Strategy

**Why TikTok works for nail art:**
- Algorithm favors new creators
- Trending sounds drive views
- Short-form video is perfect for nail tutorials
- Younger demographic (16-35)
- Viral potential is high

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Trending nail designs (quick transitions)
- 30% Tutorials (step-by-step)
- 20% Trending audio (lip-sync, dance with nails)
- 10% Behind-the-scenes

**Posting Schedule:**
- Post 3-5 videos per day (yes, daily!)
- Best times: 6-10 AM, 7-11 PM
- Consistency matters more than quality

### Content Ideas

**Video Templates:**

**Quick Transition (15-30 sec):**
```
Scene 1: Plain nail
Transition: Quick cut/effect
Scene 2: Finished nail design
Text overlay: "Design name"
Sound: Trending audio
Caption: "POV: You just got [Design] nails 💅"
```

**Tutorial (30-60 sec):**
```
Scene 1: Intro (nail artist showing design)
Scene 2: Step-by-step application
Scene 3: Finished look on hand
Scene 4: Close-up detail
Text: "How to apply [Design]"
Sound: Trending or tutorial audio
Caption: "Link in bio to shop 🛍️"
```

**Trending Audio (30-60 sec):**
```
Use trending TikTok audio
Show nail designs synced to beat
Quick cuts/transitions
Text: "Which design? 1, 2, 3, or 4?"
Caption: "All available now 💅"
```

**Before/After (15 sec):**
```
Scene 1: Plain nail
Scene 2: Finished design
Text: "Before vs After"
Sound: Satisfying audio
Caption: "Which one? 👇"
```

### Hashtag Strategy

**Use 3-5 hashtags per video:**
- #NailArt
- #NailTutorial
- #NailDesigns
- #FYP (For You Page)
- #ForYou

**Trending hashtags:** Check FYP page daily for what's trending

### Growth Tactics

1. **Post consistently** (3-5 videos/day)
   - Algorithm rewards consistency
   - More videos = more chances to go viral

2. **Use trending sounds**
   - Check Discover page for trending audio
   - Use within first 3 days of trend
   - Duets and stitches with popular creators

3. **Engage with community**
   - Comment on other nail art videos
   - Respond to all comments
   - Go live weekly

4. **Collaborate**
   - Duet with other nail artists
   - Stitch popular nail videos
   - Tag other creators

5. **Create series**
   - "Design of the day"
   - "Nail fail vs nail win"
   - "Customer requests"
   - "Trending vs classic"

   - Profile photo: Nail'd logo

### Board Strategy

**Create these boards:**
1. **Nail Designs** (primary)
   - Description: "Trending nail art designs and inspiration"
   - Pins: Your product designs

2. **Nail Tutorials**
   - Description: "Step-by-step nail art tutorials"
   - Pins: DIY nail tutorials

3. **Nail Inspiration**
   - Description: "Nail art inspiration and trends"
   - Pins: Trending nail designs (yours + others)

4. **Nail Care**
   - Description: "Nail care tips and products"
   - Pins: Aftercare kit content

5. **Customer Nails**
   - Description: "Beautiful nails from our customers"
   - Pins: User-generated content

### Pin Strategy

**Pin Design:**
- Vertical format: 1000x1500px (optimal)
- High contrast colors
- Clear, readable text
- Brand logo/watermark

**Pin Template:**
```
Top 30%: Your nail design (high quality photo)
Middle 40%: Design name + price
Bottom 30%: Call to action ("Shop Now" or "Get This Design")
Watermark: Nail'd logo
```

**Pin Text Overlay:**
- Design name
- Price
- "Shop Now"
- Trending keyword

### Content Strategy

**Posting Schedule:**
- Pin 5-10 pins per day
- Spread throughout the day
- Mix your content with curated content (50/50)

**Content Mix:**
- 50% Your nail designs
- 25% Nail tutorials
- 15% Trending nail inspiration
- 10% Nail care tips

### Hashtag/Keyword Strategy

**Use keywords in:**
- Pin title
- Pin description
- Board name
- Board description

**Keywords to target:**
- "Nail designs"
- "Nail art"
- "Trending nails"
- "Nail inspiration"
- "DIY nails"
- "Nail tutorials"
- "Nail wraps"
- "Nail subscription"

### Growth Tactics

1. **Rich Pins** (Verified website)
   - Add structured data to website
   - Pins show real-time pricing
   - Higher click-through rate

2. **Create idea pins**
   - Multi-image carousel
   - Video pins
   - Tutorial pins

3. **Collaborate**
   - Join group boards
   - Collaborate with nail artists
   - Cross-promote with complementary brands

4. **Drive traffic**
   - Every pin links to website
   - Use UTM parameters to track
   - Optimize landing pages
