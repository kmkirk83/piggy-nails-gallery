# Return & Refund Policy - Nail'd

**Last Updated:** March 2026

---

## 1. Overview

At Nail'd, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This policy outlines our return and refund procedures for different product types.

---

## 2. Return Window

### Subscription Boxes
- **Return Window:** 30 days from delivery
- **Condition:** Unopened, unused, in original packaging
- **Reason:** Defective product or not as described

### One-Time Purchases (Nail Designs)
- **Return Window:** 14 days from purchase
- **Condition:** Digital products cannot be returned (non-refundable)
- **Exception:** Defective or corrupted file

### Aftercare Kits
- **Return Window:** 30 days from delivery
- **Condition:** Unopened, unused, in original packaging
- **Reason:** Defective product or not as described

### Design Studio Subscriptions
- **Return Window:** 7 days from purchase
- **Refund:** Full refund if no designs created
- **No refund:** If designs have been created or downloaded

---

## 3. Refund Eligibility

### Eligible for Refund
- Product is defective or damaged
- Product does not match description
- Product was not delivered
- Duplicate charge
- Unauthorized purchase

### Not Eligible for Refund
- Change of mind (for one-time purchases)
- Opened or used products (physical items)
- Digital products that have been downloaded
- Designs created in design studio
- Shipping costs (unless our error)
- Customized or personalized items

---

## 4. Subscription Cancellations & Refunds

### Cancellation Policy
- You can cancel your subscription at any time
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period
- No refund for the current billing period
- You retain access until the end of the billing period

### Refund for Cancellation
- **No refund** for current billing period
- **No refund** for partial months
- **Refund available** if charged twice for same period

### Reactivation
- You can reactivate your subscription at any time
- Reactivation takes effect immediately
- You will be charged on your next billing date

---

## 5. How to Request a Return or Refund

### Step 1: Contact Support
Email: support@nailed.app

Include:
- Order number
- Reason for return/refund
- Photos of defective product (if applicable)

### Step 2: Approval
We will review your request within 2-3 business days and respond with approval or denial.

### Step 3: Return Shipping (Physical Items)
If approved, we will provide a return shipping label. You can:
- Print the label and attach to package
- Drop off at any carrier location
- Schedule pickup (if available)

### Step 4: Refund Processing
Once we receive and inspect your return:
- Refund will be processed within 5-10 business days
- Refund will be issued to original payment method
- You will receive email confirmation

---

## 6. Defective Products

### What Qualifies as Defective
- Damaged or torn nail wraps
- Incorrect size or fit
- Missing items from box
- Corrupted digital file
- Expired products

### How to Report
1. Contact support@nailed.app within 7 days of delivery
2. Include photos of defective product
3. Provide order number
4. Describe the defect

### Resolution
- We will replace the item at no cost
- Or issue a full refund
- You may keep the defective item (no return shipping required)

---

## 7. Damaged Shipments

### If Package Arrives Damaged
1. Take photos of damage
2. Contact support@nailed.app within 24 hours
3. Include order number and photos
4. Do not discard packaging

### Resolution
- We will replace the item at no cost
- Or issue a full refund
- File claim with carrier if necessary

---

## 8. Lost or Missing Packages

### If Package Never Arrives
1. Wait 5 business days after estimated delivery
2. Contact support@nailed.app
3. Provide order number and tracking number
4. We will investigate with carrier

### Resolution
- We will replace the item at no cost
- Or issue a full refund
- Typically resolved within 10-15 business days

## 9. Duplicate Charges

### If You Were Charged Twice

3. Include payment confirmation

### Resolution
- We will investigate within 2-3 business days
- Issue refund for duplicate charge within 5-10 business days
- Refund issued to original payment method

## FAQ

### Q: Can I return a subscription box?
**A:** Yes, if unopened and within 30 days of delivery. Once opened, items cannot be returned unless defective.

### Q: Can I get a refund for a digital design?
**A:** No, digital products are non-refundable once downloaded. Exception: Corrupted files will be replaced.

### Q: How long does a refund take?
**A:** Typically 5-10 business days after we receive your return, plus 3-5 business days for your bank to process.

### Q: Can I cancel my subscription and get a refund?
**A:** Subscriptions are cancelled at the end of the current billing period. No refund for the current period.

### Q: What if my package never arrived?
**A:** Contact us within 5 days of estimated delivery. We'll investigate and replace or refund.

### Q: Can I exchange instead of getting a refund?
**A:** Yes! Contact support to arrange an exchange for a different product.

### Q: Do you refund shipping costs?
**A:** Shipping refunds only if we made an error or item is defective. Return shipping is your cost unless we're at fault.

### Q: What if I was charged twice?
**A:** Contact support immediately. We'll investigate and refund the duplicate charge within 5-10 business days.
