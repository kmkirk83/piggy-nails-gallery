# Launch Marketing Campaign Guide

## Campaign Overview

Your launch campaign has three phases: Pre-Launch (Week 1), Launch (Week 2), and Post-Launch (Week 3+). This guide provides copy, graphics, and posting schedules.

## Phase 2: Launch Week (Week 2)

### Email Campaign

**Daily emails (Monday-Friday):**

**Monday: Launch recap**
```
Subject: "Thank you for launching with us! 💅"
Content: Thank you message, highlight bestsellers, share customer testimonials
```

**Tuesday: Design spotlight**
```
Subject: "This design is trending 🔥"
Content: Feature one design, show how to apply, link to shop
```

**Wednesday: Tutorial**
```
Subject: "How to apply nail wraps (step-by-step)"
Content: Video tutorial, tips and tricks, link to products
```

**Thursday: Social proof**
```
Subject: "See what our customers are creating 💅"
Content: Customer photos, testimonials, encourage UGC
```

**Friday: Weekly special**
```
Subject: "Friday flash sale - 15% off this weekend"
Content: Limited time offer, create urgency, link to shop
```

### Social Media Campaign

**Instagram:**
- Post 5-6 times per week
- Mix of product showcases, tutorials, customer features
- Stories daily
- Reels 2x per week

**TikTok:**
- Post 5-10 videos per day
- Nail design transitions
- Tutorials
- Trending audio
- Customer features

**Pinterest:**
- Pin 10 designs per day
- Spread throughout day
- Link to product pages

**Facebook:**
- Post 3-4 times per week
- Longer-form content
- Community engagement
- Customer testimonials

### Influencer Outreach

**Identify micro-influencers (10k-100k followers):**
- Search hashtags: #NailArt, #NailDesigns, #NailInfluencer
- Look for engaged audiences
- Check if they align with brand

**Outreach template:**

```
Subject: Nail'd - Free subscription box for review

Hi [Influencer Name],

We're launching Nail'd, a premium nail art subscription box, and

What's included:

• Lifetime design studio access

Let me know if you're interested!

Best,
[Your Name]
Nail'd
```

## Phase 3: Post-Launch (Week 3+)

### Email Campaign

**Weekly newsletter (every Friday):**
- Featured design
- Customer spotlight
- Nail care tip
- Special offer

**Promotional campaigns:**
- Monthly themes (Spring Nails, Summer Vibes, etc.)
- Holiday campaigns
- Product launches
- Customer appreciation

### Social Media Campaign

**Consistent posting schedule:**
- Instagram: 4-5x per week
- TikTok: 5-10x per day
- Pinterest: 10x per day
- Facebook: 3-4x per week

**Content mix:**
- 40% Product showcases
- 30% Educational (tutorials, tips)
- 20% User-generated content
- 10% Behind-the-scenes

### Paid Advertising (Optional)

**Facebook/Instagram Ads:**
- Budget: $5-10/day
- Target: Women 18-45, interested in beauty
- Objective: Website conversions
- Ad creative: Product photos, testimonials, tutorials

**Google Ads:**
- Budget: $5-10/day
- Target: "Nail art designs", "Nail subscription"
- Objective: Website traffic
- Ad copy: Highlight unique value (custom studio, trending designs)

## Contingency Plans

### If Traffic is Low
- Increase paid advertising budget
- Reach out to influencers for features
- Run flash sales to create urgency
- Post more frequently on social media

### If Conversion Rate is Low
- Review checkout flow for friction
- Add customer testimonials to homepage
- Create urgency (limited time offer)
- Improve product descriptions
- Add FAQ section

### If Email List Growth is Slow
- Increase incentive (20% discount instead of 10%)
- Add signup form to more pages
- Create exit-intent popup
- Run social media ads to email signup
