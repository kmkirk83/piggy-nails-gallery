# Nail'd - Product Fulfillment Providers (One-Time Purchases)

## Overview

Product fulfillment is for your **one-time purchases** (nail art collections, aftercare kits, individual products) rather than subscription boxes. These companies specialize in print-on-demand, dropshipping, and custom product fulfillment with branding options.

## INTEGRATION WITH YOUR NAIL'D WEBSITE

### **How It Works:**
1. **Customer places order** on your Nail'd site
2. **Order data sent via webhook** to fulfillment partner
3. **Product printed & packaged** with your branding
4. **Tracking number returned** to your site
5. **Customer receives** branded package

### **Technical Requirements:**
- Webhook endpoint at `/api/fulfillment/webhook`
- Order data includes: product ID, design, shipping address, customer info
- Tracking updates sent back to your database
- Email notifications to customer with tracking

### **Your Nail'd Site Already Supports:**
✅ tRPC procedures for order creation
✅ Database for tracking fulfillment status
✅ Webhook handler for real-time updates
✅ Email notifications for customers

## COMMON QUESTIONS

**Q: Can I use multiple fulfillment partners?**
A: Yes! Use Printify for standard products, Apliiq for premium packaging, Gelato for international.

**Q: What if I want to hold inventory?**
A: Switch to a 3PL like ShipBob or eFulfillment Service (covered in separate guide).

**Q: Can they handle custom designs?**
A: Yes, all support uploading custom nail art designs for printing.

**Q: What about returns/refunds?**
A: Each partner has different policies. Clarify before signing up.

**Q: How long does setup take?**
A: 1-2 days for API integration, 3-5 days for full testing.
