## Week 1: Account Setup & Product Curation

### Day 1-2: CJ Dropshipping Setup
- [ ] Create CJ Dropshipping account at https://cjdropshipping.com
- [ ] Complete business profile
- [ ] Add payment method (credit card or PayPal)
- [ ] Download CJ mobile app for order management
- [ ] Join CJ WhatsApp support group for 24/7 help

**Time:** 1-2 hours  
**Cost:** Free (no setup fees)

### Day 3-4: Product Curation
- [ ] Browse CJ's nail products catalog
- [ ] Select 15 trending products (mix of):
  - 5 nail wraps/polish products
  - 5 press-on nails
  - 3 accessories (rhinestones, decals, tools)
  - 2 care products (base coat, remover)
- [ ] Create product comparison spreadsheet
- [ ] Take screenshots of top 15 products

**Time:** 2-3 hours  
**Products to find:**
- Gel nail kits with LED lamp
- Press-on false nails (various designs)
- Nail art rhinestones & decals
- Manicure tool sets
- Nail polish removers

### Day 5: Test Order
- [ ] Place test order of 50-100 units (mix of products)
- [ ] Estimated cost: $200-300
- [ ] Select express shipping (7-10 days)
- [ ] Document order details:
  - Order date
  - Expected delivery date
  - Total cost

**Time:** 30 minutes  
**Cost:** $200-300 + shipping

### Day 6-7: HUIZI Contact
- [ ] Visit https://huizi.com
- [ ] Click "Get a Free Quote" button
- [ ] Fill out inquiry form:
  - Business name: Nail'd
  - Product interest: Custom nail wraps

- [ ] Request free samples (2-3 designs)

**Time:** 1 hour  
**Cost:** Free (samples included)

## Week 2: Product Quality Assessment

### Day 8-10: CJ Test Order Arrives
- [ ] Receive and inspect CJ test order
- [ ] Quality check each product:
  - Packaging condition (rate 1-5)
  - Product quality (rate 1-5)
  - Design accuracy (rate 1-5)
  - Overall satisfaction (rate 1-5)
- [ ] Document findings in spreadsheet
- [ ] Take photos of best/worst products
- [ ] Identify top 10 products to keep

**Time:** 2 hours  
**Target:** Average quality score 4.0+

## Critical Success Factors

1. **Quality Control** - Inspect every shipment
2. **Fast Fulfillment** - Ship within 48 hours of order
3. **Customer Communication** - Update customers on status
4. **Product Variety** - Rotate designs monthly
5. **Social Proof** - Collect reviews & testimonials
6. **Exclusive Designs** - Differentiate with HUIZI products
7. **Competitive Pricing** - Monitor competitor pricing

---

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Low quality products | Inspect all shipments, get customer feedback |
| Supplier delays | Maintain 2-week buffer stock |
| Customer churn | Rotate designs, offer loyalty rewards |
| Shipping issues | Use tracked shipping, offer insurance |
| Payment failures | Set up retry logic, email reminders |
| Negative reviews | Respond quickly, offer replacements |
