## Recommended Strategy for Nail'd

### Phase 1: Launch (Months 1-3)
**Provider:** Printful + Universal Fulfillment
- **Printful:** Handle one-time purchases and print-on-demand nail wraps
- **Universal Fulfillment:** Manage subscription box fulfillment with custom branding
- **Advantage:** No inventory risk, test market demand, establish brand presence

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-12)
**Provider:** SunDance (Primary) + Printful (Secondary)
- **SunDance:** Move to custom packaging manufacturing and fulfillment
- **Printful:** Continue one-time purchases and international orders
- **Advantage:** Better margins, premium packaging, scalability

### Phase 3: Scale (Year 2+)
**Provider:** SunDance (Primary) + Nimbl (Secondary)
- **SunDance:** Full custom packaging and subscription fulfillment
- **Nimbl:** Backup fulfillment for peak seasons
- **Advantage:** Maximum control, premium branding, operational redundancy
