# Nail'd - Premium Nail Art Subscription & E-Commerce Shop

## Project Overview
A premium print-on-demand nail art subscription and e-commerce platform featuring trending designs from TikTok and Instagram. Customers can subscribe to monthly boxes, purchase individual nail wraps, and buy aftercare kits. Full Stripe integration for payments.

## Product Catalog Structure

### Subscription Boxes
1. **Starter Box** - 1 month - $34.99
   - 3 nail wrap kits (20 wraps each)
   - Mini nail files
   
2. **Trendsetter Box** - 3 months - $99.99
   - 3 nail wrap kits per month
   - Mini nail files
   - Exclusive access to new designs
   
3. **VIP Box** - 6 months - $189.99
   - 4 nail wrap kits per month
   - Premium nail files
   - Exclusive designs
   - Priority customer support
   
4. **Elite Box** - 12 months - $360.00
   - 4 nail wrap kits per month
   - Premium nail files
   - Exclusive designs
   - Priority support
   - Free aftercare kit (3 months)

### One-Time Purchases
- Individual nail wrap kits: $12.99-$16.99
- Design collections: $24.99-$34.99
- Limited edition sets: $29.99-$39.99

### Aftercare Kits
- Basic Kit: $14.99 (base coat, top coat)
- Premium Kit: $24.99 (base, top, remover, file, buffer)
- Deluxe Kit: $34.99 (complete care set + nail art pen)
